About us

Hello — are you looking for a reliable, local electrician?

A B Electrical & Maintenance is a Midlands-based electrical and maintenance team. For over 13 years, our team has carried out a range of domestic and commercial work for individuals, landlords and businesses throughout Nottinghamshire, Derbyshire and the surrounding area.

Quality and attention to detail are non-negotiable in our world, as are respect, safety and trust. Our customers depend on us to do a thorough and compliant job, which is why we are committed to delivering the standard of service and finish we expect to see in our own homes.
