About us

Hello — are you looking for a reliable, local electrician?

A B Electrical & Maintenance is a Midlands-based electrical and maintenance team. For over 25 years, our team has carried out a range of domestic and commercial work for individuals, landlords and businesses throughout Nottinghamshire, Derbyshire and the surrounding area.

About this policy

This policy explains how A. B. Electrical & Maintenance uses cookies and similar technologies (such as your browser's local storage) on this website. Cookies and local storage are small pieces of data saved on your device that help a website function and remember your choices.

How we use them

We keep things deliberately minimal. This website uses only essential and functional storage. We do not use any analytics, tracking, profiling or advertising cookies, and we do not share this information with third parties for marketing.

You can clear or block cookies and local storage at any time through your browser settings — most browsers also let you do this per website. Blocking functional storage won't stop the site working, but it means your preferences (like your theme choice) won't be remembered between visits.
